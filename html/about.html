About Us

Let's Go Nepal Pvt. Ltd was founded by a team of professional mountain guiders with a decade long experience in adventure tourism. It is officially registered with the Ministry of Tourism, government of Nepal and associated to different national tourism– related organizations – Trekking Agencies Association of Nepal (TAAN), Nepal Mountaineering Association (NMA), Nepal Tourism Board (NTB) Kathmandu Environmental Educational Project (KEEP). We are located at kathmandu,Nepal, the tourist hub.The company has its own reputation in the local as well as global tourism market for its professionalism, reliability and quality services.
